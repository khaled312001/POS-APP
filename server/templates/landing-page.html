Everything you need

A Complete POS Ecosystem

From the first transaction to enterprise scale — Barmagly covers every corner of your business operations.

🛒

Smart POS Terminal

Lightning-fast checkout with barcode scanning, custom modifiers, pizza topping builder, delivery/dine-in/takeaway modes, and cash or card payment.

📦

Inventory Management

Real-time stock tracking, low-stock alerts, batch management with expiry dates, supplier purchase orders, and automatic reorder points.

👥

Employee & Shift Management

PIN-based employee authentication, role permissions (admin/manager/cashier), shift tracking with opening/closing cash counts, and attendance logs.

💳

Payment Gateway

Integrated Stripe payments with card, NFC/contactless, TWINT, and cash. 3-copy auto-print invoices (customer, kitchen, delivery). Full refund support.

📊

Analytics & Reports

Daily, weekly, and monthly sales dashboards. Product performance, employee sales, tax reporting, expense tracking, and Excel exports.

🤝

Customer CRM & Loyalty

Customer profiles with purchase history, loyalty points, caller ID integration, credit balances, and birthday reminders for personalized service.

🏢

Multi-Branch Management

Manage unlimited store locations from one super-admin dashboard. Each branch with its own inventory, staff, and performance metrics.

🔒

Enterprise Security

Device-bound license keys, role-based access control, JWT authentication, encrypted data storage, and automatic daily encrypted backups.

🌍

Multi-Language Support

Full RTL Arabic, English, and German support throughout the entire app — menus, receipts, invoices, notifications, and settings.
